# GTM — Go-To-Market Strategy


## Executive Summary

The target market is engineering managers and CTOs working for startups with budgets ranging between $5K/month to $50K/month for AI, and that really doesn't know if they're being overcharged. They simply pay whatever price their AI tools charge without evaluating their expenditure.

Their biggest weakness: they're all on Hacker News, Product Hunt, and Cursor/AI forums where they talk about tool costings. This is 10X more affordable for our marketing than conventional SaaS marketing tactics.

The pitch is straightforward: "Have your tools audited, in 30 seconds, no email necessary. You get your emails after you see the savings, which we'll then connect to Credex."  

---

## 1. Target User

### Primary Persona
- **Title:** Engineering Manager, HRs, Tech Lead, Founder, Hr and CTO
- **Company Size:** 5–50 people  
- **Company Stage:** Seed or Series A 
- **Monthly AI Spend:** $3k–$25k 
- **Real Problem:** People don't compare the cost of AI tools according to need.
- **Actual Pain Points:**
  - "We use Cursor, Claude, and ChatGPT but nobody  manages the budget and use it in optimized way"
  - "Accounting says we spent $8k last month on AI. Is that normal?"
  - "I switched 3 people to Claude Team but forgot to cancel their Pro accounts"
  - "The Employees are overusing AI because  of lack of skill and monitoring"

## 4. First 100 Users Strategy

- Find 3-5 people in my network who manage teams.

- Ask they for imporvement and suggestions and apply them in project .
- Ask them to share if they like it
