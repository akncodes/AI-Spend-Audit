## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Do This
- ✅ Speak to real pain (budget meetings, overspending, waste)
- ✅ Use concrete numbers ($500, $2k, exact savings)
- ✅ Be honest (small savings case: "You're spending well")
- ✅ Short sentences (mobile-friendly)
- ✅ Action verbs (Audit, Discover, Find, See)

### Don't Do This
- ❌ Generic tech speak ("leveraging AI," "synergies")
- ❌ Vague benefits ("optimize," "maximize")
- ❌ Manufactured urgency ("limited time offer")
- ❌ Making up stats ("97% of startups overspend")
